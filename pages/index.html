BMW E36

The BMW 3-series is the third generation of the 3-series cars of the German automaker BMW, produced from 1990 to 2000. Models of this generation became larger, received an elegant streamlined body and were originally created as four-door, as opposed to the mainly two-door cars of the second generation.
